## 中文摘要

《SIAM 数学分析杂志》，第 58 卷，第 3 期，第 2067-2110 页，2026 年 6 月。摘要。众所周知，偏微分方程 (PDE) 的可控性与伴随系统的可观测性不等式的证明密切相关，有时，这涉及分析与所考虑的 PDE 相关的谱问题。在这项工作中，我们研究了一系列光谱问题，以确保星图上著名的 Korteweg-de Vries 方程的可控性。这项研究归结为确定与该谱问题相关的某些函数何时完整。这里的新颖之处在于在星图结构的背景下呈现这种详细的分析。

## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2067-2110, June 2026.   Abstract. It is well known that the controllability property of partial differential equations (PDEs) is closely linked to the proof of an observability inequality for the adjoint system, which, sometimes, involves analyzing a spectral problem associated with the PDE under consideration. In this work, we study a series of spectral issues that ensure the controllability of the renowned Korteweg-de Vries equation on star-graph. This investigation reduces to determining when certain functions, associated with this spectral problem, are entire. The novelty here lies in presenting this detailed analysis in the context of a star graph structure.
