# Lifespan Bounds and Super-Classical Propagation for Weak Solutions of the Three-Dimensional Compressible Euler Equations

- **作者 (Authors)**: Thomas C. Sideris
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-22
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20290v1)

## 中文摘要

我们在 $[0,T)\times\mathbb R^3$ 上建立了三维等熵可压缩欧拉方程的有界弱解的寿命上限，在初始数据的局部正性条件下，并假设扰动的基本支持传播到未扰动的背景状态的速度不快于经典局部适定性的预测。我们进一步表明，任何具有有界逆密度且持续超出此上限的熵允许有界弱解必须以严格的超经典速度传播到未受干扰的区域，并且这种加速传播必然伴随着解的相关单边 $L^\infty$ 轮廓中的跳跃不连续性。

---

## 英文摘要

We establish an upper bound on the lifespan of bounded weak solutions to the three-dimensional isentropic compressible Euler equations on $[0,T)\times\mathbb R^3$, under a localized positivity condition on the initial data and the assumption that the essential support of the disturbance propagates into an undisturbed background state no faster than predicted by classical local well-posedness. We further show that any entropy-admissible bounded weak solution with bounded inverse density that persists beyond this upper bound must propagate into the undisturbed region at a strictly super-classical speed and that this accelerated propagation is necessarily accompanied by a jump discontinuity in an associated one-sided $L^\infty$-profile of the solution.
