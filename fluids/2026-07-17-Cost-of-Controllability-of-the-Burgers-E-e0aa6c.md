# Cost of Controllability of the Burgers Equation Linearized at a Steady Shock in the Vanishing Viscosity Limit

## 中文摘要

《SIAM 数学分析杂志》，第 58 卷，第 3 期，第 2702-2737 页，2026 年 6 月。摘要。我们考虑在稳态冲击下线性化的一维 Burgers 方程，并通过左端点的控制来研究其零可控性成本。我们给出了该成本保持在消失粘度极限内所需的控制时间的上限和下限，并且我们构造了具有显式极限行为的可接受的控制。我们还提供了对控制作用于两个端点的情况的分析扩展。该证明依赖于复杂的分析，并采用了以前用于解决具有恒定传输项的同一问题的方法。

## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2702-2737, June 2026.   Abstract. We consider the one-dimensional Burgers equation linearized at a stationary shock and investigate its null-controllability cost with a control at the left endpoint. We give an upper and a lower bound on the control time required for this cost to remain bounded in the vanishing viscosity limit, and we construct an admissible control with an explicit limit behavior. We also provide an extension of the analysis to the case where the control acts on both endpoints. The proof relies on complex analysis and adapts methods previously used to tackle the same issue with a constant transport term.
