## 中文摘要

以下内容是 2026 年第一学期在澳大利亚国立大学举办的专题讲座的一部分。那是一次非常好的经历，我真的很喜欢这个为期 12 周（每周 2 小时）的讲座。它是自包含的，从傅立叶变换和索博列夫空间的结果开始。作为一个玩具模型，在处理纳维-斯托克斯系统之前，我们首先关注非线性热方程，其中非线性为多项式。最终，我们证明了临界空间中纳维-斯托克斯方程温和解的存在性和唯一性。该脚本包含一些练习以及期中考试和期末考试的文本。

## 英文摘要

The content of the following pages was part of a special topics lecture given at the Australian National University during the first semester of 2026. That was a very nice expericence, I really enjoyed giving this 12 weeks (2 hours a week) lecture. It is meant to be self contained, starting with results on Fourier transform and Sobolev spaces. As a toy model, before treating the Navier-Stokes system, we focus on the non linear heat equation where the non linearity is polynomial. Ultimately, we prove existence and uniqueness of mild solutions of the Navier-Stokes equations in critical spaces. This script contains some exercises and the text of the mid-semester exam as well as the final exam.
