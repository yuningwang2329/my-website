# An $L^p$-theory for global weak solutions to the Navier-Stokes equations in exterior domains

- **作者 (Authors)**: Filippo Palma
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-23
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.21282v1)

## 中文摘要

在本文中，我们考虑勒贝格空间 $L^p$、$p\in (2,3)$ 中初始数据的外域纳维-斯托克斯初始边值问题，并证明全局弱解的存在性。对于所引用的解决方案，我们还提供了一个结构定理。特别是，我们看到弱解在某一时刻之后变得规则，并且它也是规则的a。 e.及时。尽管局部强/弱解的通用 $L^p$ 理论在文献中已经很成熟，但外部域中全局弱解的相应理论似乎是新的。当然，我们的结果在柯西问题和半空间中的初始边值问题的特定情况下成立。

## 英文摘要

In this paper, we consider the Navier-Stokes initial boundary value problem in exterior domains with initial data in the Lebesgue space $L^p$, $p\in (2,3)$, and show the existence of a global weak solution. For the quoted solution, we also furnish a structure theorem. In particular, we see that the weak solution becomes regular after a certain instant of time and it is also regular a. e. in time. Although a general $L^p$-theory for local strong/mild solutions is well-established in the literature, a corresponding theory for global weak solutions in exterior domains seems to be new. Of course, our results hold in the particular cases of the Cauchy problem and the initial boundary value problem in a half-space.
