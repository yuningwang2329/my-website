# Well-posedness of a Hele-Shaw problem in general dimensions

- **作者 (Authors)**: Olga Turanova, Yuming Paul Zhang
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-22
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20395v1)

## 中文摘要

我们研究一般空间维度中的 Hele-Shaw 型自由边界问题。我们使用粘度流的概念建立了该问题的比较原理和适定性。我们工作的一个重要特点是我们的假设允许自由边界速度取负值。

---

## 英文摘要

We investigate a Hele-Shaw type free boundary problem in general spatial dimension. We establish a comparison principle and well-posedness for the problem using a notion of viscosity flows. An important feature of our work is that our hypotheses allow for the free boundary velocity to take negative values.
