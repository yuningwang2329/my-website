## 中文摘要

摘要
                  根据 Kapovitch 和 Wilking 的工作，具有非负 Ricci 曲率的闭流形具有一致几乎为零的基本群。剩下的问题和猜想，询问在这种情况下基本群实际上是否一致地几乎是阿贝尔群。这项工作的主要目标是构造具有一致正的里奇曲率的示例，其基本群不能一致地几乎是阿贝尔的。

## 英文摘要

ABSTRACT
                  It follows from the work of Kapovitch and Wilking that a closed manifold with nonnegative Ricci curvature has a uniformly almost nilpotent fundamental group. Leftover questions and conjectures, have asked if in this context the fundamental group is actually uniformly almost abelian. The main goal of this work is to construct examples  with uniformly positive Ricci curvature  whose fundamental groups cannot be uniformly virtually abelian.
