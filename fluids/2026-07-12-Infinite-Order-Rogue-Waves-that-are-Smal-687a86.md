                  无限阶的一般流氓波构成了聚焦非线性薛定谔方程的一系列解，这些解最近在各种渐近极限中被识别，例如贝克伦德变换的高阶迭代和具有特定幅度分布的脉冲的半经典聚焦。这些解决方案具有令人信服的特性，例如有限[公式：参见文本]范数，与缺乏相干结构的情况下异常缓慢的时间衰减形成对比。在本文中，我们研究了参数极限下无限阶一般流氓波的渐近行为，其中解在紧集上均匀变小，而[公式：参见文本]范数保持固定。我们证明，解主要集中在对数重新缩放的时空坐标中的特定曲线的一侧，并且我们根据椭圆函数以及调制孤子的形式获得了该区域中解的前导渐近行为。即使解变得均匀小，渐近公式也能捕获固定的[公式：参见文本]-范数。

## 英文摘要

Abstract.
                  General rogue waves of infinite order constitute a family of solutions of the focusing nonlinear Schrödinger equation that have recently been identified in a variety of asymptotic limits such as high-order iteration of Bäcklund transformations and semiclassical focusing of pulses with specific amplitude profiles. These solutions have compelling properties such as finite [Formula: see text]-norm contrasted with anomalously slow temporal decay in the absence of coherent structures. In this paper we investigate the asymptotic behavior of general rogue waves of infinite order in a parametric limit in which the solution becomes small uniformly on compact sets while the [Formula: see text]-norm remains fixed. We show that the solution is primarily concentrated on one side of a specific curve in logarithmically rescaled space-time coordinates, and we obtain the leading-order asymptotic behavior of the solution in this region in terms of elliptic functions as well as near the boundary curve in terms of modulated solitons. The asymptotic formula captures the fixed [Formula: see text]-norm even as the solution becomes uniformly small.
