# Self-consistent bounds method for dissipative PDEs

- **作者 (Authors)**: Daniel Wilczak, Piotr Zgliczynski
- **来源 (Source)**: J. Differ. Equ.
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-17
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0022039626005504?dgcid=rss_sd_all)

## 中文摘要

我们讨论具有周期性边界条件的耗散偏微分方程的自洽边界方法。我们证明了一类耗散偏微分方程的伽辽金投影的收敛定理，该定理构成了构建算法的一般框架的理论基础，该算法计算基础偏微分方程的解的界限及其对初始条件的依赖性。我们还表明，抛物线偏微分方程的经典例子，包括 Kuramoto-Sivashinsky 方程和圆环上的纳维-斯托克斯方程都适合这个框架。

---

## 英文摘要

We discuss the method of self-consistent bounds for dissipative PDEs with periodic boundary conditions. We prove convergence theorems of Galerkin projections for a class of dissipative PDEs, which constitute a theoretical basis of a general framework for construction of an algorithm that computes bounds for the solutions of the underlying PDE and its dependence on initial conditions.We also show, that the classical examples of parabolic PDEs including Kuramoto-Sivashinsky equation and the Navier-Stokes on the torus fit into this framework.
