# The Bogoliubov–Bose–Hubbard Model: Existence of Minimizers and Absence of Quantum Phase Transition

- **作者 (Authors)**: Norbert Mokrzański, Marcin Napiórkowski
- **来源 (Source)**: SIAM J. Math. Anal.
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-17
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/abs/10.1137/25M1746914?af=R)

## 中文摘要

《SIAM 数学分析杂志》，第 58 卷，第 3 期，第 2138-2185 页，2026 年 6 月。摘要。我们考虑基于 Bogoliubov 理论的 Bose-Hubbard 模型的变分方法。我们引入了大正则和正则自由能泛函，并证明了极小值的存在性。通过分析它们的结构，我们通过证明系统在足够低的温度下是超流体并且在高温下是绝缘的来证明热驱动相变的存在。特别是，我们证明该模型不表现出量子相变。

## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2138-2185, June 2026.   Abstract. We consider a variational approach to the Bose–Hubbard model based on Bogoliubov theory. We introduce the grand canonical and canonical free energy functionals for which we prove the existence of minimizers. By analyzing their structure we show the existence of a thermally driven phase transition by showing that the system is superfluid at sufficiently low temperatures and insulating at high temperatures. In particular, we show that this model does not exhibit a quantum phase transition.
