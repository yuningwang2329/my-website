## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2031-2066, June 2026.   Abstract. We derive a linearized version of the monotonicity method for shape reconstruction using time harmonic elastic waves. The linearized method provides an efficient version of the method, drastically reducing computation time. Here we show that the linearized method has some additional advantages. The linearized method in particular can be used to obtain additional information on the material parameters, and it is able to partially separate and identify the supports of the Lamé parameters.
