# Hall–MHD System as a Simplified One-Fluid Ion Model Derived from Two-Fluid Euler–Maxwell Equations

- **作者 (Authors)**: Yue-Jun Peng, Shuai Xi, Liang Zhao
- **来源 (Source)**: SIAM J. Math. Anal.
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-17
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/abs/10.1137/25M1733033?af=R)

## 中文摘要

《SIAM 数学分析杂志》，第 58 卷，第 3 期，第 2578-2612 页，2026 年 6 月。摘要。我们从部分可压缩的双流体欧拉-麦克斯韦系统中推导出单流体离子模型，其碰撞机制中离子被认为是可压缩的，而电子则不是。由于电场的奇异性和洛伦兹力的强耦合效应，仅基于零电子质量极限或准中性极限推导这样的模型是相当开放的。在这项工作中，我们应用了零电子质量和准中性极限的组合。我们证明了全局时间存在性和光滑解的组合极限。此外，对于准备充分的初始数据，我们建立了一流体和二流体模型的平滑解之间的收敛误差估计。值得一提的是，本研究中离子和电子的速度是不同的。

## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2578-2612, June 2026.   Abstract. We derive the one-fluid ion model from the partial-compressible two-fluid Euler–Maxwell system with a collision mechanism in which the ions are considered compressible while electrons are not. Deriving such a model based solely on the zero-electron-mass limit or the quasi-neutral limit is quite open because of the singularities of the electric field and the strong coupling effects of the Lorentz forces. In this work, we apply the combined zero-electron-mass and quasi-neutral limit. We prove the global-in-time existence and the combined limit of smooth solutions. Furthermore, for well-prepared initial data, we establish the error estimates of the convergence between smooth solutions to one-fluid and two-fluid models. It is worth mentioning that the velocities of ions and electrons are different in this study.
