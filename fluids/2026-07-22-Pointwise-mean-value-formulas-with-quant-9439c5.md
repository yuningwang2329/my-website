# Pointwise mean-value formulas with quantitative remainder for higher-order Poisson equations

- **作者 (Authors)**: Fernando Charro, Sophia Hertrich, Catherine Lebiedzik
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-22
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20776v1)

## 中文摘要

高阶泊松方程涉及拉普拉斯算子的整数次幂和非零强迫项，出现在物理和工程领域，例如流体动力学、结构工程和图像处理。我们引入了一系列用于高阶泊松方程解的平均值公式，以迭代均值的线性组合形式给出，并通过强迫项的振荡来量化精确的余数。我们还证明了正则性结果以及与均值性质的强逆，即仅满足我们的公式的局部可积函数是高阶泊松方程的正则解。与均值属性强制平滑的齐次情况相反，这里可达到的规则性由强制项的规则性决定。总之，我们的结果提供了高阶泊松方程解的平均值特征。

## 英文摘要

Higher-order Poisson equations involve an integer power of the Laplacian and a nonzero forcing term, and appear in areas of physics and engineering such as hydrodynamics, structural engineering, and image processing. We introduce a family of mean-value formulas for solutions to higher-order Poisson equations, given in terms of linear combinations of iterated means, with an exact remainder quantified by the oscillation of the forcing term. We also prove a regularity result and a strong converse to the mean-value property, in the sense that merely locally integrable functions satisfying our formulas are regular solutions of the higher-order Poisson equation. In contrast with the homogeneous case, where the mean-value property forces smoothness, the regularity attainable here is dictated by the regularity of the forcing term. Together, our results provide a mean-value characterization of solutions to higher-order Poisson equations.
