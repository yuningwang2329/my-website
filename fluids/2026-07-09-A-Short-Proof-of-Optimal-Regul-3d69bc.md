# A Short Proof of Optimal Regularity for minimizers of the Alt-Phillips Problem

**日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-09

## 中文摘要

我们给出了 $γ\in (0, 1)$ 的 Alt-Phillips 自由边界问题最小化的最优正则性的独立简短证明。我们采用源自[DS20]的二分法论证。

---

## 英文摘要

We give a self-contained short proof of optimal regularity for minimizers of the Alt-Phillips Free Boundary Problem for $γ\in (0, 1)$. We adopt a dichotomy argument that originates from [DS20].
