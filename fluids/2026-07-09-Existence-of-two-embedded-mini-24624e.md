# Existence of two embedded minimal spheres in $S^3$ with an arbitrary metric

**作者 (Authors)**: Zhichao Wang, Xin Zhou
**来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
**日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-09
**原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08631v1)

## 中文摘要

我们证明赋予任意黎曼度量$g$的$S^3$允许至少两个嵌入的最小球体。该证明基于相对最小-最大构造的迭代方案。

---

## 英文摘要

We prove that $S^3$ endowed with an arbitrary Riemannian metric $g$ admits at least two embedded minimal spheres. The proof is based on an iterative scheme of relative min-max constructions.
