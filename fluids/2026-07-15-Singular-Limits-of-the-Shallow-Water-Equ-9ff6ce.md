# Singular Limits of the Shallow Water Equations on the Sphere

- **作者 (Authors)**: Bin Cheng, Steve Schochet
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-15
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14257v1)

## 中文摘要

当弗劳德数与罗斯贝数之比有界时，快速旋转球体上的微可压缩浅水方程的解被证明是一致有界的。此外，在奇异极限中，这些参数的比率保持固定，同时它们都趋于零，具有充分准备的初始数据的解趋向于极限方程的相应解。对于三尺度奇异极限也得到了收敛结果，其中弗劳德数比罗斯贝数更快趋于零。

---

## 英文摘要

Solutions of the slightly compressible shallow water equations on a rapidly rotating sphere are shown to be bounded uniformly when ratio of the   Froude number to the Rossby number is bounded. Moreover, in the singular limit in which the ratio of those parameters remains fixed while they both   tend to zero, solutions with well-prepared initial data tend to corresponding solutions of limit equations. A convergence result is also obtained   for the three-scale singular limit in which the Froude number tends to zero faster than the Rossby number.
