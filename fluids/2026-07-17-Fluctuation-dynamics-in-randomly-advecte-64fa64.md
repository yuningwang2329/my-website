# Fluctuation dynamics in randomly advected Navier-Stokes equations below critical scaling

- **作者 (Authors)**: Arnaud Debussche, Martina Hofmanová
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-17
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16132v1)

## 中文摘要

我们研究随机平流不可压缩纳维-斯托克斯方程，其中平流场是具有平滑一阶相关性的零均值、无散度、时空平稳速度场。我们引入了一个双参数模型族，其中平流在快速时间尺度 $\varepsilon^2$ 上加速，并且具有空间相关长度 $δ$；临界状态 $\varepsilon = δ$ 对应于纳维-斯托克斯方程的自然抛物线标度。在完全亚临界状态 $\varepsilon = o (δ)$ 中，我们证明了维度 $d = 2, 3$ 中的大数定律：解收敛到确定性纳维-斯托克斯系统，其扩散系数由 Green-Kubo 公式给出。在二维空间中，在稍强的假设 $\varepsilon = o (δ^{1 + ι})$ 下，对于某些 $ι> 0$，我们确定了主阶涨落：在减去满足纳维-斯托克斯型非线性系统的确定性宏观校正后，重新缩放的涨落收敛于高斯场，求解由乘性时空白噪声驱动的线性化纳维-斯托克斯方程。

## 英文摘要

We study randomly advected incompressible Navier-Stokes equations, where the advecting field is a mean-zero, divergence-free, space-time stationary velocity field with smooth order-one correlations. We introduce a two-parameter family of models in which the advection is accelerated on a fast temporal scale $\varepsilon^2$ and has spatial correlation length $δ$; the critical regime $\varepsilon = δ$ corresponds to the natural parabolic scaling of the Navier-Stokes equation. In the full subcritical regime $\varepsilon = o (δ)$, we prove a law of large numbers in dimensions $d = 2, 3$: the solutions converge to a deterministic Navier--Stokes system with an enhanced diffusion coefficient given by a Green-Kubo formula. In two space dimensions, under the slightly stronger assumption $\varepsilon = o (δ^{1 + ι})$ for some $ι> 0$, we identify the leading-order fluctuations: after subtracting deterministic macroscopic corrections satisfying a nonlinear system of Navier-Stokes type, the rescaled fluctuations converge to a Gaussian field solving a linearized Navier-Stokes equation driven by multiplicative space-time white noise.
