## 中文摘要

《SIAM 数学分析杂志》，第 58 卷，第 3 期，第 2439-2474 页，2026 年 6 月。摘要。我们在[math]上构造了一系列不可压缩欧拉方程的经典涡度解，构成了一般行进点涡系统的[math]型正则化。该构造是通过在[数学]上应用切线映射和李雅普诺夫-施密特约简论证来完成的。通过引入归一化条件来处理[数学]上的旋转不变性，我们证明涡流将位于基尔霍夫-劳斯函数的一维简并归一化临界点附近。此外，在每个涡中心的切线空间中，缩放流函数被验证为广义等离子体问题的基态扰动。本文还获得了正则化级数的其他一些定性和定量估计。

## 英文摘要

SIAM Journal on Mathematical Analysis, Volume 58, Issue 3, Page 2439-2474, June 2026.   Abstract. We construct a series of classic vorticity solutions for the incompressible Euler equation on [math], which constitute the [math] type regularization for a general traveling point vortex system. The construction is accomplished by applying a tangent mapping on [math] and the Lyapunov–Schmidt reduction argument. By introducing a normalized condition to deal with the rotation invariance on [math], we prove that the vortices will be located near a 1-dimensional degenerate normalized critical point of the Kirchhoff–Routh function. Moreover, in the tangent space at each vortex center, the scaled stream function is verified as a perturbation of the ground state for a generalized plasma problem. Some other qualitative and quantitative estimates for the regularization series are also obtained in this paper.
