# Failure of the Least Action Admissibility Principle in the Context of the Compressible Euler Equations

- **作者 (Authors)**: Simon Markfelder, Valentin Pellhammer
- **来源 (Source)**: SIAM J. Math. Anal.
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-21

## 中文摘要

抽象的。
                  为多维正压可压缩欧拉方程及相关系统寻找合适的解概念仍然是一个未解决的问题。正如凸积分所揭示的，可接受的弱解（也称为弱熵解）的经典概念不会导致唯一性，并且允许看似不物理的解。为此，人们研究了额外的标准，以排除凸积分产生的反直觉解的能力。最近，在[H. Gimperlein、M. Grinfeld、R. J. Knops 和 M. Slemrod：最小作用允许原则，Arch。配给。机甲。 Anal., 249 (2025), Paper No. 22, https://doi.org/10.1007/s00205-025-02094-z]，有人建议将最少行动可接受性原则作为所需的选择标准。然而，在本文中，我们表明，最小动作可接受性原则排除了直观上与物理相关的解决方案。因此，人们要么必须重新考虑自己的直觉，要么必须放弃最小行动可接受性原则。

## 英文摘要

Abstract.
                  Finding a proper solution concept for the multidimensional barotropic compressible Euler equations and related systems is still an unsolved problem. As revealed by convex integration, the classical notion of admissible weak solutions (also known as weak entropy solutions) does not lead to uniqueness and allows for solutions which do not seem to be physical. For this reason, people have studied additional criteria in view of their ability to rule out the counterintuitive solutions generated by convex integration. Recently, in [H. Gimperlein, M. Grinfeld, R. J. Knops and M. Slemrod: The least action admissibility principle, Arch. Ration. Mech. Anal., 249 (2025), Paper No. 22, https://doi.org/10.1007/s00205-025-02094-z], it was suggested that the least action admissibility principle serves as the desired selection criterion. In this paper, however, we show that the least action admissibility principle rules out the solution which is intuitively the physically relevant one. Consequently, one either has to reconsider one’s intuition, or the least action admissibility principle must be discarded.
