# Incompressible Navier-Stokes limit of non-bilinear kinetic equations and application to the BGK, nonlinear Fokker-Planck and Boltzmann-Fermi-Dirac equations

- **作者 (Authors)**: Pierre Gervais
- **来源 (Source)**: Arxiv (math.AP)
- **日期 (Date)**: 2026-07-21
- **原文链接 (Link)**: [查看原始论文](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18939v1)

## 中文摘要

我们考虑碰撞算子不一定是双线性的碰撞动力学方程，并证明加权索博列夫空间中纳维-斯托克斯-傅里叶系统的定量收敛性，以及初始层的描述。本文的目的是将 Bardos-Golse-Levermore 的抽象动力学方程的条件收敛结果与 Bardos-Ukai 发起的玻尔兹曼方程谱策略相结合，以保持宏观量和耗散熵。这项工作将仅限于双线性碰撞（Boltzmann、Landau 或其他模型的二次近似）的 Gervais-Lods 抽象方法扩展到非双线性模型，例如 Boltzmann-Fermi-Dirac 方程、BGK 方程和非线性 Fokker-Planck 方程。

---

## 英文摘要

We consider collisional kinetic equations whose collision operator is not necessarily bilinear and prove quantitative convergence to the Navier-Stokes-Fourier system in weighted Sobolev spaces, together with a description of the initial layers. The aim of this paper is to conciliate the conditional convergence result of Bardos-Golse-Levermore for abstract kinetic equations conserving macroscopic quantities and dissipating entropy with the spectral strategy initiated by Bardos-Ukai for the Boltzmann equation. This work extends the abstract approach of Gervais-Lods which was restricted to bilinear collisions (Boltzmann, Landau or quadratic approximation of other models) to non-bilinear models such as the Boltzmann-Fermi-Dirac equation, the BGK equation and the nonlinear Fokker-Planck equation.
